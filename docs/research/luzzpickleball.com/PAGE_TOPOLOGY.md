# LuzzPickleball Homepage Topology

## Overall Flow

1. Announcement strip
2. Transparent overlay header
3. Full-width hero carousel
4. Dark testimonial marquee
5. Spotlight Paddles tabbed product section
6. `Luzz Go!` editorial block
7. Warranty banner
8. Video review card rail
9. `Explore Our Collection` five-card mosaic
10. Universal collaboration split section
11. FAQ accordion
12. LUZZ TEAM gallery
13. Footer

## Layout Notes

- The header overlays the hero rather than occupying normal document flow.
- The hero and marquee act as the visual introduction and should feel tightly stacked.
- Mid-page sections alternate between white and light gray backgrounds.
- Dense commercial sections are separated with generous vertical spacing and large all-caps headings.

## Interaction Model by Section

- Announcement strip: time-driven
- Header: mostly static on the homepage capture
- Hero: time-driven + manual next/previous
- Testimonial strip: time-driven marquee
- Spotlight Paddles: click-driven tab switch
- Luzz Go: static
- Warranty: static
- Video reviews: horizontally scrollable / carousel-like
- Collection grid: hover-driven emphasis
- Universal collaboration: hover-driven emphasis
- FAQ: click-driven accordion
- Team gallery: drag/scroll driven
- Footer: static
